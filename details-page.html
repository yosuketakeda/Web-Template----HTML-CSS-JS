What we'll do

First of all, if you don’t see a date or time available that will work for you and a partner let me know when would be good and I’ll try to set something up!
We will have a short warm up and intro on acro-etiquette, then your education will begin. This will be a fun session in which we explore our capabilities in the world of AcroYoga. We will be at a gorgeous beach cliff location and will take as many photos as you want!

So this is how it goes functionally:

All poses will be demonstrated and broken down for bases, flyers, and spotters.

By the end of our time you will learn:

how to base, fly and spot foundational postures
how to access stability when basing and flying through bone stacking and core engagement
how to spot correctly for each posture and cultivate a safe space to grow within
how to communicate effectively and build trust with your partners
how to connect learned foundational postures into a final flow
Your host: Annie Smith
AcroYoga is so much FUN! Also it provides needed health benefits of physical activity, and human interaction, trust, and communication.
I practice and teach AcroYoga everyday because I love it so much. I really enjoy the joy I see in others when they discover that this activity is possible and accessible to most people. I will teach all you aspiring AcroYogis out there how to base and fly safely.
